** Tournament Team Sorter **
Created by:	Tony Ayala
Last revised:	11/3/2015

Files required:
players.txt - Should have list of players attending tournament.
*Format for player per line* (no quotes)
"player name", "division name"

ranks.txt - List of ranks and given MMR values.
*Format for rank/mmr per line* (no quotes)
"division name", "mmr value"

Files created:
extras.txt - Extra players in tournament.

teams.txt - List of teams and players in tournament.
